A real-time journey through the first landing on the Moon
Introduction

Upon starting the application, select whether to begin one minute before launch, or click "Now" to drop in exactly 50 years ago, to-the-second during the anniversary. Media (film, TV, photography, and audio) appears as it was collected during Apollo 11.

Navigate to any moment of the mission using the time navigator at the top of the screen. The top bar is the entire mission with two bars below it providing magnification. Selecting transcript items, photos, commentary items, or guided tour moments, also jumps the mission time to the moment they occurred.

Selecting a Mission Control audio channel opens the Mission Control view panel, and plays the "live" audio of that Mission Control position. Change channels by selecting the seats in mission control. These 50 channels of audio have only recently been digitized and restored, and have never before been made publicly available. They total over 11,000 hours in length.
